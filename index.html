Kaan Dıgdıgoğlu, also known as Digdigson, is a composer, music producer, and guitarist from Türkiye, working across film, trailers, games, and commercial media. From intimate emotional storytelling to large-scale cinematic soundscapes, Kaan's distinctive hybrid style blends orchestral depth with modern electronic production, creating music that feels both human and powerful.

Having played guitar from an early age, Kaan developed a deep passion for orchestration and the emotional language of sound. Today, he produces high-quality, orchestra-driven music across a wide range of genres, combining expressive live performance with contemporary production techniques.

He currently works alongside multi-award-winning composer Rahman Altın as his assistant, taking part in film and media productions while gaining first-hand experience in the creative and technical sides of professional scoring.

Kaan is also one half of the Half Past Midnight music-for-media duo, collaborating with producer Seyit "Lidez" Güvenen to create emotionally driven, cinematic works that merge hybrid orchestration with modern production.

His music has been featured in short films, trailers, commercials, and game projects, marked by a strong commitment to storytelling and refined sonic detail.
